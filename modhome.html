Mod Home is a quick and affordable design solution which allow for growth. The assembly system is built from pre-fabricated, sustainable, compressed bamboo-mat boards. These structures are dismantlable and can be sold as products, not requiring building permits.

Product Opportunity


Environment friendly alternative to concrete pre-fabricated construction.

Structures can be dismantled and transported. Parts are manufactured at the factory and assembled on site.

Legally considered a temporary structure and does not require building sanctions.



Concept

The house unit consists of 8 cubes, each cube designed to accommodate a specific function, in entirety. The edge length of each cube is 2.5m(8ft); the height required for a structure to be usable.

Panel Fabrication

Each face/wall of a cube consists of 2 panels, each 4ftX8ft. A panel is designed as a wall, half-wall, door or window panel.

The panels consist of two outer skins sandwiching a rigid Polyurethane Foam (PUF). Bamboo boards are one of the materials used as a skin. Multiple bamboo mats are compressed and infused with resin to produce boards of varying thickness.

The panels are strong enough to be used without structural members for supports. They are strong and sustainable.
